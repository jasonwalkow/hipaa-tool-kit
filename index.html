HIPAA Tool Kit

Designed to help providers meet HIPAA rules and regulations, this all-in-one package is an ideal resource for covering your HIPAA compliance needs.

What does the HIPAA Tool Kit Include?

HIPAA Policies & Procedures

All HIPAA Tool Kit Policies and Procedures are in-line with current regulations, including HIPAA's Security Rule, Privacy Rule, Enforcement Rule, Omnibus Rule, and all other regulatory actions.

Individual Policies and Procedures include, but are not limited to, Notice of Privacy Practices, Use of PHI for TPO, Third Party Disclosures, Authorization for Use or Disclosure, Tracking Logs, Covered Entities and Business Associates, as well as Breaches and Breach Risk Assessment.

HIPAA Training

Live Training

Once you have purchased the HIPAA Tool Kit, you and your staff will be scheduled for a live initial HIPAA Compliance Training from a Certified Compliance Consultant.

Training Materials

In addition to the Live Training, you will receive training materials for new hires and ongoing education. Training materials are easy-to-follow and aesthetically modern.
